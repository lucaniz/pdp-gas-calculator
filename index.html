PDP Gas & Costs Calculator

This calculator aims to provide insights into the gas costs, fees, and profitability of Filecoin PDP based on on-chain activity. You can input the expected deal price, FIL price, number of roots (within fixed ranges), and ProofSet size. The tool compares profitability across different scenarios using High, Low, and Average (last 30 days) base fee values.

Gas consumption estimates are based on empirical measurements. In essence:

  • ProvePossession for a proofset of 100 roots costs approximately 140M gas units.
  • ProofSet size does not significantly impact gas usage.
  • Increasing the number of roots increases cost by ~10M per 10x more roots.
