PDP Gas & Costs Calculator

This calculator provides insights into Filecoin PDP gas costs, fees, and profitability. Adjust deal price, FIL price, number of roots, and proof set size. Gas values are based on empirical data and on-chain analysis.

Gas consumption estimates are based on empirical measurements:

  • ProvePossession for a proofset of 100 roots costs ≈ 140 M gas.
  • Adding more roots increases cost by ~10 M per 10× roots.
  • NextProvingPeriod adds 55 M gas (kept in this version).
  • Gas for AddRoots is excluded.

Data references:

  • PDP Verifier empirical gas data: Google Sheet
  • Base fee data from Starboard.
  • Methodology discussion: Filecoin Slack thread
